Who Am I?

Hello, traveler! I'm the kobold behind this site—just your friendly neighborhood lore-keeper and creator. By day, I hang out with my cat Tibbles and Day dream up new worlds; by night, I tinker with character ideas and write down the wildest homebrew. Whether you're a veteran adventurer or a curious newcomer, you're welcome here at the hearth.

Why Did I Make This Website?

The Cozy Kobold is my little corner of the internet, where I share my love for tabletop RPGs, homebrew ideas, and storytelling magic. I wanted a space where the community can come together to explore, create, and celebrate the joy of D&D. Think of it as a cozy tavern where ideas flow as freely as the drinks!

How Can You Support the Cozy Kobold?

If you enjoy what you find here—whether it's a quirky race, a new class, or just a spark of inspiration—feel free to share the site with fellow adventurers! Your support helps keep the torches lit and the kettle warm. Stay cozy, and keep creating!
